About The Team

The Markham Space Invaders are a high school robotics team located in Markham, Ontario, comprised of students from across the region. We provide students with the opportunity to explore robotics through the FIRST Robotics Program. The team was established with the objectives of promoting and raising awareness of STEM, creating a positive and stimulating environment for inventors, and providing a community forum to explore opportunities and apply skills in a competitive environment.
